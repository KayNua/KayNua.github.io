TAKE ACTION!

There are some potential solutions to help advocate for teachers pay raise. Below are some suggestions for you to get involved.

What Can We Do?

You can advocate for higher teacher salaries by educating others through social media, brochures, websites, or telling your friends, family, teachers, guardians, etc.


  • National Education Association . "NEA advocate for change and for a $40,000 minimum salary for all preschool through grade 12 teachers." You could join teachers when they go on strikes and protest to push for policy changes.
  • Worthy Wage Campaign . This campaign advocates for quality care for children, better education, training, and higher salaries for teachers. You should participate in Worthy Wage Day. You could also email or write a letter to your school boards,state and federal government.
